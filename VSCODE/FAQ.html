Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about our made-to-order process, delivery, returns, and payment options.

Made to Order

1. Are all products made to order?

Yes. All Azure Haven Home pieces are made to order to ensure careful craftsmanship, refined finishing, and a more premium furniture experience.

2. Why are your items made to order?

Our made-to-order process allows us to focus on quality, detail, and thoughtful production instead of mass-made inventory. This helps us deliver furniture that feels more intentional and well-crafted.

3. Can I cancel my order after placing it?

No. Orders are strictly non-cancellable once confirmed because each piece is made specifically for the customer.

Shipping

4. Do you ship nationwide in the Philippines?

Yes. We deliver nationwide within the Philippines.

5. How long does shipping take?

Orders are prepared and shipped within 8 days from order confirmation. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and courier schedule.

6. How can I track my order?

Once your order has been processed and shipped, tracking details can be provided through your order confirmation or follow-up message. You may also contact us directly for updates.

Returns

7. Do you accept returns?

Yes. We offer a 30-day return guarantee for eligible items with verified issues such as damage, defects, or incorrect items received.

8. What items are eligible for return?

Returns are accepted for orders with verified product problems, including damage during delivery, manufacturing defects, or incorrect item fulfillment.

9. Can I return an item if I simply changed my mind?

No. Returns should be based on verified issues related to the product received.

Payments

10. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery and bank payments only.

11. Do you accept credit cards or e-wallets?

At this time, Azure Haven Home only accepts COD and bank payments.

12. When will I receive bank payment instructions?

Bank payment details are provided after order confirmation so you can complete your transaction securely.
